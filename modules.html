Modules

What do we do?

The modules team is in charge of designing modules, which are short explorations into data science, in which faculty offer students the opportunity to work hands-on with a dataset relevant to their course, and some instruction on the principles of statistics and computing.

Projects

Modules

The modules team is available to work with faculty to use the tools and design the module to fit the course objectives. Data science modules are as diverse as the myriad disciplines taught across Berkeley.
