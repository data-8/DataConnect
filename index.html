Data Science Education Program: Student Team

The Data Science Education Program (DSEP) is a team dedicated to improving
Data Science at UC Berkeley. Learn more about each individual team below.

  • Curriculum

    Works with professors and other subject matter experts to design future data science courses and implement resources for students

  • Diversity

    Conducts interviews, collects demographic/ethnographic data, and develops toolkits of best practices in order to foster a diverse and inclusive data science community at Berkeley

  • Infrastructure

    Develops web infrastructure/tools to illuminate and share the efforts of the data science community at Berkeley

  • Mapping

    Collects data and creates tools to better understand the Data Science ecosystem at Berkeley, including its strengths and its connections across research, teaching, and student pathways

  • Outreach

    Guides students across campus and develops best practices in designing, producing, and distributing information on the Data Science Education Program to the student community

  • Project

    Compiles, organizes, and manages a database of data science projects for Berkeley students to get involved in
