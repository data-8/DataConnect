Data Scholars

What do we do?

Data Scholars is a student-led program intended to address the issues of underrepresentation in the data science community. The program was founded on the belief that anyone can become a data scientist and use the concepts taught in Data 8 in any field to make a difference.

Projects

Advising

Join a vibrant data science community and participate in social events, talks, and more.

Mentorship

The program offers mentorship from graduate students and post-docs to guide scholars.


Training

The program offers exclusive workshops in Python, statistics, probability, and machine learning as well as specialized office hours and group tutoring sessions.
