Connector Associates

What do we do?

The Connector Associate team works to provide support to the instructors and students involved in Data8 Connector Courses. Each team member helps a specific course instructor write labs, assist in class, develop overall course content. Our team is also working on collecting course material to make it available as a resource to future instructors teaching under the Data Science Realm.
